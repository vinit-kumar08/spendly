Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly's core features, we collect the information you give us directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account. We also collect the expense data you choose to enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you attach to a transaction.

In addition, we may automatically collect basic technical information, such as your browser type and general usage patterns within the app, to help us understand how Spendly is used and to fix problems.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to operate and improve Spendly, including to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Display, categorize, and summarize your expenses within the app
  • Diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the service
  • Communicate with you about your account or important updates

We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our application database and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or legitimate operational reasons.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party providers for infrastructure needs such as hosting, analytics, or email delivery. These providers only process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes. We do not share your expense details with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through the contact details provided in the app, and we'll be glad to help.
